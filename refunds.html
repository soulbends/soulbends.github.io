Refund and Service Extension Policy

At Soulbends, we are committed to your satisfaction and strive to offer the highest quality yoga and wellness services to our community. We understand, however, that our service may not be the right fit for everyone. For this reason, we have a clear and straightforward refund policy.

Refund Policy

If you are not satisfied with the service you have received, you are eligible to request a full refund.

  • A request for a refund must be made within 3 days from your date of purchase and signup.
  • To initiate a refund, please send an email to us at yogadaily@soulbends.com with your purchase details and the reason for your request.
  • Once your request is received, the refund will be processed to your original method of payment within 5 to 7 working days.

Service Extension Policy

In the event that you experience significant technical issues from our end or are unsatisfied with the quality of service delivery, you may request a service extension.

  • This policy lasts for 3 days. A request for a service extension must be made within 3 days of your service start date or the date of the specific session in question. If 3 days have passed, we unfortunately cannot offer you a service extension.
  • To request a service extension, please send us an email at yogadaily@soulbends.com detailing the issue. Our team will review the request and take appropriate action.
